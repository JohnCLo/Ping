Hey there! We're a new startup that is focused to provide our customers a cheap, fast delivery service. Aw man, not another delivery service! That's a fair response, but with delivery services charging upwards of $5 plus hidden price inflations, taking at least 50 minutes, and closing services at 9pm, we here at Pronto feel we can provide a better service.

How? A combination of lower transportation costs, localization, and algorithmic magic all serve to enforce our mission statement: to get from A to B as fast and as cheaply as possible. So if you're interested, we'd love for you to participate in our closed beta!

Again, how? We've probably already contacted you personally about beta-testing our service and we'll download the latest stable version of our app onto your iPhone or Android device. The app is currently not published on the Apple App Store or Android Marketplace. We'll also be sending a few very short surveys regarding our performance, pricing, and user interface experience.

Thanks for helping us out!
